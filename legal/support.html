Common questions

How do I start a trip?

Open the app, tap New trip, name it, add who’s coming, and add each city with its dates. Voyage builds the day-by-day itinerary for you.

How do I share a trip with someone?

Inside a trip, use the share option to invite a fellow traveller by email, or create a public recap link to share highlights. You can remove access at any time.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your trips are tied to your account and only visible to you and anyone you explicitly invite. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Open Your trips and choose Delete account. This permanently removes your account, trips, journal and photos. It’s immediate and can’t be undone.

Why is the weather missing or wrong?

Weather comes from a third-party service based on the destination you entered. It can occasionally be unavailable or imprecise — please don’t rely on it for safety-critical plans.
