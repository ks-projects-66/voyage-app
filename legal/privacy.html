Privacy Policy

Effective 29 June 2026

Voyage (“we”, “us”) is a travel-planning app that turns your destinations and dates into a day-by-day companion. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We keep it deliberately small: Voyage is built to hold your trips, not to profile you.

What we collect

  • Account details. Your email address and a securely hashed password, used to sign you in and sync your trips across devices.
  • Trip content you create. Trip names, destinations and dates, the names of travellers you add, itinerary and places, logistics notes, and journal entries — including any text, ratings and photos you choose to add.

We do not collect your device’s precise location, we do not use advertising, and we do not run third-party analytics or tracking. Weather is based on the destination names you type, not on where your device is.

How we use it

Only to provide the app: to build your itinerary, show weather for the places you’re visiting, keep your journal, and sync everything to your account. We don’t use your content for anything else.

Service providers

  • Supabase — hosting, authentication, database and photo storage. Your account and trip data live here, on servers in the EU. (Supabase privacy)
  • Open-Meteo — weather and place lookup. We send destination city names and coordinates to fetch forecasts; no personal data is sent. (Open-Meteo terms)
  • Maps — the app can open Google Maps in your browser for directions or to find a place. That’s a normal web link and is governed by Google’s own policies.

Sharing

  • Shared trips. If you invite someone to a trip, they can see and contribute to that trip’s content.
  • Public recap links. If you create a shareable recap link, anyone with that link can view the recap until you remove it. This is entirely opt-in.
  • We never sell your data, and we don’t share it with advertisers.

Keeping and deleting your data

We keep your data while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app: open Your trips and choose Delete account. Deletion is immediate and irreversible — your trips, journal, photos and sign-in are removed.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit. Access is enforced by row-level security, so each person can only reach their own trips and trips that have been explicitly shared with them.

Children

Voyage isn’t directed at children under 16, and we don’t knowingly collect their information.

Changes

If we update this policy, we’ll revise the effective date above.
